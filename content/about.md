I am **Fangyuan Shi**, a graduate student pursuing an **M.S. in Operations Research** at the
**Georgia Institute of Technology**. My work centers on **data-driven decision-making in
operational systems** — building models that turn noisy, real-world data into decisions, and
studying when classical optimization, statistical, and machine-learning tools each earn their
keep.

Before Georgia Tech, I completed a **B.Eng. in Industrial Engineering** at **Sichuan
University** in China. Across both programs I have been drawn to problems where rigorous
modeling meets messy data: large-scale optimization and decomposition, time-series
forecasting, physics-informed simulation, and — more recently — the use of large language
models as general-purpose optimizers.
